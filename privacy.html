Privacy Policy for Bastion Web Blocker

Effective Date: September 14th 2024

Bastion Technology Services ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Bastion Web Blocker Chrome extension (the "Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Extension.

Information We Collect

  1. User-Provided Information:

    When you use the Extension, you may provide us with information such as:

    • Websites you choose to block.
    • Admin password (if set by you).
  2. Storage:

    The Extension uses the Chrome storage API to save your preferences, including the list of blocked websites and the admin password. This information is stored locally in your browser and is not shared with us or any third parties.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the functionality of the Extension.
  • To allow you to customize your browsing experience by blocking specified websites.
  • To manage user preferences and settings.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties.

The Extension does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or track your browsing activity.

Permissions

The Extension requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • declarativeNetRequest: To block specified websites as per your preferences.
  • storage: To save your blocked sites and settings.
  • tabs: To detect tab updates and change icons based on tab status.

Security

We take the security of your information seriously. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mail@bastiontech.org .
